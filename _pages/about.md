I am an incoming **Master's student in Computer Science** at the **National University of Singapore (NUS)** (2026.8 – 2028.7), graduating from **Shanghai University of Finance and Economics (SUFE)** with a B.S. in Business Analytics.

My research and engineering interests center on **Large Language Models (LLMs)**, **Multimodal Representation Learning**, **AI Agents**, and their applications in **Finance & Content Safety**. I enjoy bridging cutting-edge model research with real-world industrial systems.

I am currently working with the **Tencent Youtu AI Lab** on LLM safety and multimodal research, with the **SUFE FinAI Center** (advised by Prof. **Liwen Zhang, 张立文**) on financial reasoning and agent safety, with **Shanghai Jiao Tong University** (advised by Prof. **Xiaodong Gu, 顾小东**) on **LLM for Code**, and with the **Head Office of ICBC (Private Banking Department)** on scientist-discovery agents.

Research Interests
======
- **Large Language Models** — SFT / GRPO / PPO, reasoning, distillation, alignment
- **Multimodal Models** — vision–language–video embeddings, retrieval, fusion
- **AI Agents & RAG** — tool use, planning, agent benchmarks for finance
- **AI for Finance** — financial reasoning, agent safety, knowledge graphs
- **Content Safety** — multi-label moderation, low-resource & few-shot detection

Selected Highlights
======
- 📝 **FinVault** — *Benchmarking Financial Agent Safety in Execution-Grounded Environments* — co-author, **submitted to EMNLP 2026**. [arXiv:2601.07853](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.07853)
- 🛠️ **ARH (AI Research Helper)** — open-source CLI-first research assistant agent with tool use, three-layer memory, skill self-learning and multi-LLM fallback. [github.com/LiRunGuo/Arhelper](https://github.com/LiRunGuo/Arhelper)
