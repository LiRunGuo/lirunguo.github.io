I am an incoming **Master's student in Computer Science** at the **National University of Singapore (NUS)** (2026.8 – 2027.7), graduating from **Shanghai University of Finance and Economics (SUFE)** with a B.S. in Business Analytics (GPA 3.73/4.0, ranked 8/128).

My research and engineering interests center on **Large Language Models (LLMs)**, **Multimodal Representation Learning**, **AI Agents**, and their applications in **Finance & Content Safety**. I enjoy bridging cutting-edge model research with real-world industrial systems.

I am currently working with the **Tencent Youtu AI Lab** on adversarial data synthesis and unified content moderation models, with the **SUFE FinAI Center** on the open-source financial reasoning model **Fin-R1**, and with the **Head Office of ICBC (Private Banking Department)** on scientist-discovery agents.

Research Interests
======
- **Large Language Models** — SFT / GRPO / PPO, reasoning, distillation, alignment
- **Multimodal Models** — vision–language–video embeddings, retrieval, fusion
- **AI Agents & RAG** — tool use, planning, agent benchmarks for finance
- **AI for Finance** — financial reasoning, agent safety, knowledge graphs
- **Content Safety** — multi-label moderation, low-resource & few-shot detection

Selected Highlights
======
- 🏆 **Fin-R1** — co-developer of China's first open-source financial reasoning LLM (7B). FinEval score **75.2** (2nd place overall).
- 🏆 **WeMM-Embedding** — multimodal embedding model reaching open-source SOTA on **MMEB-V2** (0.7523) and **UVRB** video retrieval (0.686).
- 🏆 **Medusa Moderation Model** — unified content-safety model used in production: **92.3%** accuracy, **−35%** false positives, **+60%** review efficiency, hundreds of thousands QPS.
- 📝 **FinVault** — *Benchmarking Financial Agent Safety in Execution-Grounded Environments* — first co-author, under review at **ACL 2026**.
