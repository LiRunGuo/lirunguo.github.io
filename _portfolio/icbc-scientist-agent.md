A joint project with the **Head Office of the Industrial and Commercial Bank of China (ICBC), Private Banking Department**, building an Agent + RAG system to support the bank's scientist-entrepreneur business.

### Approach
- LLM-based parsing of patents, papers, news, registries to extract structured signals.
- Unified **scientific knowledge graph** linked with enterprise data.
- **RAG-based expert knowledge base** for grounded reasoning.
- Family-business opportunity insight, dialogue sparring, and one-customer-one-strategy marketing planning.

### Outputs
- Multi-criteria scientist discovery (by domain, level, region).
- Panoramic profiles across four dimensions: person / firm / family / society.
- Affiliated-enterprise analysis and automatic marketing-graph generation.
